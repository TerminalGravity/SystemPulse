# Privacy Policy for System Pulse

**Last Updated: January 17, 2026**

## Overview

System Pulse is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your system information belongs to you and you alone.

## Data Collection

**System Pulse does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any data.**

Specifically, we do NOT collect:
- Personal information
- System statistics or usage data
- Analytics or telemetry
- Crash reports
- Location data
- Any other information

## How It Works

System Pulse reads your Mac's system information (CPU, memory, battery, etc.) using standard macOS APIs and displays it locally on your screen. This information:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not stored persistently
- Is not transmitted over any network
- Is refreshed every 2 seconds and immediately discarded

## Third-Party Services

System Pulse does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## App Sandbox

The Mac App Store version of System Pulse runs in Apple's App Sandbox, providing an additional layer of security and privacy protection.
